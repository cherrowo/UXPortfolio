As I've gotten more familiar with the project, my focus has shifted towards understanding user behaviour and applying that in the fields of data science and UX design.

Responsibilities: Content Auditing, User Research, User Centric Storytelling, Wireframing, Information Architecture Design, Usability Testing, Web Design, Visual Design

  • Resolved match issues in Firebase to improve participant experience with the new reporting feature
  • Analysed participant survey responses on Typeform to identify areas of dissatisfaction with past matches to formulate new strategies to eliminate systemic issues in the stable-matching system, decrease number of unmatched students, improve match quality, and increase signups from LGBTQ+ students
  • Managed launch at the most popular campus, accounting for 30% of the 3500+ signups acquired 12 hours after product release
  • Spearheaded exploratory data analysis using Python, Pandas, and matplotlib to understand magnitude of algorithm bias within ethnic groups, and creating tests to explore methods to reduce bias
  • Played a key role in the development of safety features, working on developing a smooth user experience, using approachable language in copywriting
